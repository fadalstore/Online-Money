### 🚀 Sida loo otomaatig gareeyo Content-ka (The "AI-Workflow"):
Maaddaama aad tahay developer, waxaad isticmaali kartaa nidaamkan si aad maqaallada u soo saarto si degdeg ah:
 1. **Mawduuc Soo xulid:** Dooro hal mawduuc oo jadwalka kore ku jira.
 2. **AI Drafting:** Isticmaal Gemini si aad u qorto "Outline" iyo "First Draft" adigoo raacaya qaabkaaga (Style-ka Fadal Store).
 3. **Optimization:** Hubi in maqaal kasta uu leeyahay **Internal Links** u geeya maqaalladii hore (Topic Cluster).
 4. **Publish:** Geli GitHub, ka dibna isla markiiba u gudbi Search Console.
