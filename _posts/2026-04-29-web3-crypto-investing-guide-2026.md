<h2 id="what-is-web3">What is Web3?</h2>
<p>Web3 is the third generation of the internet where users own their data and assets through blockchain technology. In 2026, it's not just about Bitcoin; it's about decentralized apps (dApps) and smart contracts.</p>

<h2 id="how-to-start">How to Start Safely</h2>
<ol>
    <li><strong>Educate First:</strong> Never invest in what you don't understand.</li>
    <li><strong>Use Secure Exchanges:</strong> Stick to regulated platforms.</li>
    <li><strong>Diversify:</strong> Don't put all your money into one coin.</li>
</ol>

<div class="tip-box">
    <strong>Warning:</strong> The crypto market is volatile. Only invest money you are willing to lose, and always use two-factor authentication (2FA).
</div>

<h2 id="future">The Future of Digital Assets</h2>
<p>From tokenized real estate to digital identity, the use cases for blockchain are expanding. Staying informed is your best investment strategy.</p>
