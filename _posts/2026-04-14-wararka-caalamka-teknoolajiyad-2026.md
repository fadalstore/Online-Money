## 🌐 Internetka Cusub — Starlink, 6G, iyo Web3

**Starlink (SpaceX):**
Hadda waxaa internet isticmaalaya dad ku nool meelo fog oo Afrika, Aasiya, iyo Soomaaliya xitaa ay ku jiraan. Starlink waxay gaadhsiisay internet dhakhso ah (+200 Mbps) in ka badan **90 waddan**.

**6G:**
- Shiinaha iyo Japan waxay bilaabeen tijaabada 6G
- La filayo in la bixiyo 2028–2030
- Dhakhso: 100x ka dhakhso badan 5G

**Web3 & Blockchain:**
- NFT-yada si weyn hoos ayay ugu dhaceen, laakiin blockchain-ka waa la isticmaalayaa
- Dowladaha 40+ ayaa baadhinaya Central Bank Digital Currency (CBDC)

## 🔐 Cybersecurity — Khataraha Cusub

AI-ga wuxuu kaloo faa'idaysanayaan hackers-ka. Dhacdooyinkii ugu waaweyn ee 2026:

> **Febraayo 2026:** Hab-wabaha (infrastructure) korontada Yurub ayaa la ku duulay cyber attack — Russia la sheegtay inay ka dambeyso.

> **Maarso 2026:** Shirkad ka mid ah 10ka ugu weyn adduunka ayaa la furtay xogta macaamiisha — $4.5 bilyan oo qiimo leh.

**Sida aad nafta u ilaalin karto:**
1. Password manager isticmaal (Bitwarden bilaash ah)
2. 2FA (Two-Factor Authentication) — email + app
3. VPN — gaar ahaan xaaladaha loo maro wifi-ga dadweynaha
4. Update-ka software-kaaga — dib ha u dhicin
