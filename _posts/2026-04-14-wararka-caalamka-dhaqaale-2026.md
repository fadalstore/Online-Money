## 🤖 AI iyo Shaqo-Waayaha — Runta Adag

Mid ka mid ah su'aalaha ugu weyn ee 2026: **Ma AI-ga ayaa shaqo-waayaha keenaya?**

Warbixinta McKinsey ee Maarso 2026 waxay sheegtay:

- **300 milyan oo shaqaale** adduunka ka mid ah ayaa halis ku jira shaqadooda AI darteed
- Laakiin **shaqooyin cusub** oo la filayo in ay AI-gu keento: +97 milyan 2025–2030
- Shaqooyinka ugu khatar badan: **xisaabaadka**, **xogta gelinta**, **telefoonka adeegga**
- Shaqooyinka ugu badbaad badan: **daryeelka caafimaadka**, **garsoorka**, **xirfadaha gacanla**

> **Faa'idada kugu jirta:** Haddaad barato AI tools + coding, adiga oo aan jaamacad aadan, waxaad heli kartaa shaqo $2,000–$8,000/bishii. Tani waa fursad taariikheed.

## 🪙 Lacagaha Dijitaalka (Crypto) — Xaaladda 2026

Bitcoin wuxuu galay **$95,000** Febraayo 2026 ka dib markii ETF-yada Bitcoin ay caalamka si weyn uga dhex baaheen. Laakiin waxaa jira digniinno muhiim ah:

**Wanaagga:**
- ETF-yada Bitcoin: shirkadaha waaweyn ayaa marasho weyn galiinaya
- Ethereum: smart contracts cusub oo kordhaya
- Stablecoins: ganacsiyada caalamka ayaa isticmaalaya si aad

**Khatarta:**
- Xeerarka dowladaha wali aan is waafaqsanayn
- Dib-u-dhac joogto ah ee suuqa (volatility)
- Hackers-ka iyo doorkii
