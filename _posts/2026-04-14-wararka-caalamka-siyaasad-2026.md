## 🏛️ 1. Doorashooyinka Yurub — Isbedelka Siyaasadeed

Yurub waxay 2026 ku jirtaa xilli doorasho muhiim ah. Dalalka Faransiiska, Jarmalka, iyo Isbaanishka ayaa la filayaa in ay dib u habeeyaan xukumaadooda. Xisbiyada xaq-dhawrka ah (right-wing) ayaa awood ku kordhaysa, taas oo saameyn kara siyaasadda Midowga Yurub (EU).

**Waxa la filayo:**
- Faransiiska: Doorashada baarlamaanka Juun 2026
- Jarmalka: Baarlamaankii cusub ayaa bilaabay shaqadiisa Febraayo 2026
- EU: Khilaaf cusub oo ku saabsan xeerarka socdaalka

## 🇺🇸 3. Mareykanka — Siyaasadda Gudaha

Sannadka 2026 ee Mareykanka wuxuu ku jiraa tartanka doorashooyinka baarlamaanka dhexe (midterm elections). Madaxweynaha Biden wuxuu xoojinayaa gaadiidka dowli-ahaanshaha, halka xisbiga Republican-ka uu ku hawlan yahay dib-u-soo-celinta awoodda.

> **Xogta muhimsan:** Aqalka Wakiilada (House of Representatives) ayaa weli gacanta ku jira Democrat-yada si khafiif ah, laakiin Senate-ku waa xiisow.

---

## 🇨🇳 4. Shiinaha iyo Xiisaha Taiwan

Xiisaha u dhaxeeya Shiinaha iyo Taiwan weli waa socda. Gaadiidka militariga Shiinaha ayaa la arkay agagaarka jasiiradda Taiwan si joogto ah. Maraykanka iyo Japan waxay awood militari u direen gobolka Indo-Pacific.

**Saameynta ganacsiga:**
- Shuqullada alaabada semiconductor (chip-yada) ayaa xagga caalamka saamayn wayn ku leh
- Shirkadaha tech ee dunida ayaa baadhinaya beddelka deegaanka wax-soo-saarka
