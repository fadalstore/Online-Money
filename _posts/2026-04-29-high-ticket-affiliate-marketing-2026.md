<h2 id="what-is">What is High-Ticket Affiliate Marketing?</h2>
<p>Unlike traditional affiliate marketing (like Amazon Associates) where you earn small percentages, high-ticket marketing focuses on products costing $1,000 or more. Think software enterprise plans, luxury travel, or high-end coaching programs.</p>

<h2 id="products">Best High-Ticket Products to Promote</h2>
<ul>
    <li><strong>SaaS Platforms:</strong> High-end CRM or marketing automation tools.</li>
    <li><strong>Online Masterminds:</strong> Premium educational programs.</li>
    <li><strong>Financial Services:</strong> Investment platforms and trading tools.</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="strategy">The "Bridge Page" Strategy</h2>
<p>Don't send traffic directly to the offer. Create a "Bridge Page" (like this blog) where you provide value, build trust, and then recommend the high-ticket solution.</p>

<div class="highlight-box">
    <strong>Success Secret:</strong> Focus on solving a big problem. The bigger the problem you solve, the more people are willing to pay for the solution.
