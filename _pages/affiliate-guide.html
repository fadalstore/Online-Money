🔗 Sida Xiriirka Affiliate ah Loogu Daro Website-kaaga
Tallaabada kasta oo aad raacdo marka aad affiliate link heshid
1

Program ku biir — Link gaarka ah hel

Marka aad program ku biirtid (Fiverr, Hostinger, iwm), dashboard-kaaga waxay ku siinayaan link gaarka ah — tusaale: https://www.fiverr.com/?affid=XXXXXXXX

2

Maqaallada Jira — Links ku bedel

Tag tools.html iyo maqaallada ku jira Fiverr/Upwork/Hostinger links — ku beddel affiliate link-kaaga gaarka ah. Hada links-yada generic ayay yihiin.

3

Maqaallo Cusub Qor — Affiliate Focus ah

Maqaallada "review" iyo "vs" noocahiisu waxay conversion-rate sareeya. Tusaale: Fiverr vs Upwork iyo Hostinger Review hada website-kaagu ku jira.

4

Disclosure Ku Dar (Sharci)

Maqaallada affiliate links leh waxay u baahan yihiin ogeysiis: "Maqaalkan wuxuu ku jiraa xiriirro affiliate ah — haddaad iibsatid, lacag yar ayaan helo." Fadal Store hore ayuu u leeyahay.

5

Dashboard La Soco — Clicks & Earnings

Program kasta dashboard ayuu leeyahay. Maalin kasta eeg: immisa click, immisa conversion, immisa lacag. Tani waxay kuu caawinaysaa inaad garaneyso maqaallada ugu fiican.
