💡 Sida Shaqo Remote ah Loogu Helo — Tips
Xeelado dhabta ah ee aad ku hesho shaqo online ah dhakhso
1

Profile-kaaga la mid yihiin xirfadaha laga raadinayo

Google "remote [xirfadaada] jobs 2026" — eeg erayadii inta badan jira job postings-ka, ku dar profile-kaaga.

2

Portfolio website yar samee

GitHub Pages ama Carrd.co isticmaal si aad u sameyso portfolio website bilaash ah. Shaqooyin 3–5 ah ku tus.

3

LinkedIn optimize gare

Xiddig oo Profile-kaaga, ku dar "Open to Work" banner, iyo maalin kasta 5–10 connections cusub samee xirfadahaaga meesha.

4

Proposal gaara qor — ma aha template

Marka aad shaqo codsaneyso, akhri job posting-ka oo si gaara uga jawaab. Macaamiliin templates-yada si dhakhso ah bay ku garanaanayaan.

5

La soco — 80% sales waxay dhacaan follow-up 5aad

Haddaan cid kuu jawabin, ku soo noqo 3–5 maalmood kadib. Si xushmad leh laakiin joogto ah.

Bilaabo Maanta — Ha Sugnaanin

Koorso bilaash ah ka bilow, xirfad dhis, macmiil raadi. Taasaa ugu fudud ee online income loogu helo.
