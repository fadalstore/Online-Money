📋 Checklist: Freelancer Cusub (Tallaabada 1–10)

☐
Fiverr.com account samee — magac isticmaale xirfad muujinaysa xulo
☐
Upwork.com account samee — profile 100% dhamaystir
☐
Sawir xirfadeed xidh (weji muujinaya, dhar nadiif ah)
☐
Bio qor: cidda aad tahay, waxa aad qaban karto, natiijada aad bixineyso
☐
Gig 2–3 ah samee Fiverr-ka (xirfadahaada ku saleysan)
☐
Portfolio muunad 2–3 shaqo ah soo diyaari (haddaanay jirin, samee muunado bilaash ah)
☐
Payoneer account samee si lacagta loo qaato
☐
Fiverr/Upwork ku xidh Payoneer account-kaaga
☐
20 qof oo aad garaneyso u dir fariin gaar ah oo shaqada ku saabsan
☐
Upwork: 5 proposal dir maalin kasta shaqooyinka cusub
☐
Fiverr: Online joog oo messages-ka 1 saacad gudahood ka jawaab
☐
Shaqadii ugu horeysay: Review codso macmiilka ka dib marka dhammaato

🌐 Checklist: Blog/Website Cusub

☐
Domain name dooro: gaara, xisho la'aan, .com doorso
☐
Hosting iibso: Hostinger ($2.99/bilood) ama GitHub Pages (bilaash)
☐
WordPress ama Jekyll rakibo iyo theme ku xidh
☐
Google Analytics iyo Google Search Console ku xidh
☐
SSL certificate xidh (HTTPS)
☐
Pages muhiimka ah samee: About, Contact, Privacy Policy
☐
Maqaallada ugu horeeyaa 5–10 qor kahor aad Google-ka u gudbiso
☐
Keyword research samee — ereyada dadku raadiyaan ku qor
☐
Sitemap.xml samee oo Google Search Console ku gudbiso
☐
Xawliga website-ka ku tijaabi PageSpeed Insights
☐
Affiliate links ku dar maqaallada kuu habboon
☐
Google AdSense codso marka aad 10+ maqaallo leedahay

📣 Checklist: Maqaal SEO Fiican (Ka Hor Soo Daynta)

☐
Keyword raadi — erayga ugu muhiimsan maqaalka ku jira Title-ka
☐
Title-ka: 50–60 xaraf, keyword horteeda ku jiro
☐
Meta description: 150–160 xaraf, xiiso leh, click soo jiidanaysa
☐
URL gaaban oo keyword ku jiro: /maqaalka-cinwaanka.html
☐
Sawir kasta alt text ku dar (keyword ku jiro)
☐
H2 iyo H3 headings isticmaal — keyword-yada secondary ku dar
☐
Internal links: 2–3 maqaallo kale ku xidh
☐
Dhererka: 1,000+ eray waa inuu yahay
☐
Sawirrada optimize gare (KB yar, loading dhakhso)
☐
Maqaalka soo dayn ka dib: Google Search Console ku gudbiso
